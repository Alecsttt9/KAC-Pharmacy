Terms and Conditions

Privacy Protection
KAC Pharmacy is committed to maintaining your privacy and we take our responsibility for safeguarding your Protected Health Information (PHI) very seriously. The Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) governs how KAC Pharmacy may use and disclose PHI, including information like medication history, medical conditions, and health insurance information.
How is my medication shipped?
We deliver your medications in discrete packaging. If your medication requires refrigeration it will be shipped seperately from the rest of your order in temperature-safe packaging,
What are shipping rates and times?
Premium KAC Pharmacy members get free two-day delivery. All customers receive free ground shipping, or can upgrade to tw0-day shipping for $3.99.
Are there any shipping restrictions?
Youc can ship your medications to physical addresses where KAC Pharmacy services are offered. We cannoot ship to P.O boxes, APO/FPO/DPO addresses, or lockers at this time.
What if I use multiple pickup locations?
Your prescription may be processed and medication order filled at any licensed pharmacy in our network in compliance with pharmacy regulations, including third-party licensed pharmacies. The dispensing pharmacy will always be identified on the prescription label.
How can I report an issue with KAC Pharmacy?
Our customer service team and pharmacists are available from 8am-10pm ET weekdays and 10am-8pm ET weekends. After normal business hours, a voicemail system is available for customers. Please leave a message and a pharmacist will get back to you as soon as possible.
